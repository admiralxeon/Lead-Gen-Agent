# BrightPixel Studio — Services

## Website design and redesign
We design and rebuild fast, mobile-friendly websites that load in under two seconds and look right on every screen. Ideal for businesses with outdated, slow, or non-responsive sites that lose visitors on mobile.

## Local SEO
We help local businesses rank in Google Maps and local search so nearby customers can actually find them. This includes Google Business Profile optimization, location-specific service pages, local keywords, and a review-generation strategy.

## Paid advertising
We run and manage Google Ads and Meta (Facebook and Instagram) advertising campaigns focused on measurable lead generation and return on ad spend, not vanity metrics.

## Content and copywriting
We write clear website copy, landing pages, and blog content that turns visitors into enquiries instead of leaving them confused about what a business actually does.

## E-commerce and online ordering
We add online stores, booking systems, and online ordering to existing sites, so businesses can take orders, reservations, and payments directly instead of relying only on phone calls and walk-ins.
