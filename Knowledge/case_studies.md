# Case Studies

## Bakery — added online ordering
A local bakery relied entirely on walk-in customers and phone orders. We added an online ordering and pickup system to their existing website. Within three months they were taking over 120 online orders a month — a completely new revenue stream they previously had no way to capture.

## Plumber — local SEO
A plumbing company was effectively invisible on Google outside of paid ads. We optimized their Google Business Profile and built location-specific service pages for each area they cover. They reached the first page of local search for their main service area and roughly doubled their inbound call volume.

## Law firm — mobile-first redesign
A law firm had an outdated, desktop-only website that looked broken on phones — where most of their traffic actually came from. We rebuilt it mobile-first with clear calls to action and simplified navigation. Mobile enquiries increased by about 60 percent after launch.

## Restaurant — reservation site and paid ads
A new restaurant needed bookings quickly. We built a simple menu-and-reservation website and ran a targeted local Meta ad campaign. They filled their opening month and kept a steady flow of weekend reservations afterward.
